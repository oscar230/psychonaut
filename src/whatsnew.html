August 19, 2000 - Here's a small update to tide everyone over while I work on an overhaul and larger update to the site. Several new reports (Near Disaster, Oceanic Bliss, Sex Magick and a Later Revisit, Deep Into the K-Hole, Foxy Rave) and an updated FAQ. February 22, 2000 - Well, we made it past Y2K. As many of you noticed, it has been several months since the last update. I apologize, but my life has been chaotic. I haven't had much chance to work on the site. I am uploading a batch of reports today, and working on an overhaul to the site, so keep checking in over the next few weeks. Today we have Smoked DPT my first trip report from Burning Man 99. This is followed by L's First Bees (BM 99 Trip2) where L tastes the Bees for the first time, Proposal (BM 99 Trip 3) where I propose to L and she accepts, and Acid Fox (BM 99 Trip 4) where acid and foxy methoxy meet during our last night at Burning Man. After I returned I had to try Oral DPT and a short time later I experienced the extreme weirdness of The Trance an LSD plus Salvia trip. I have lots more reports to upload in the next week or two, plus other changes. And a last note: before you congratulate me on my engagement you should know L and I broke up at the end of the year. Condolences are similarly unnecessary.
