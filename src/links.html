Links

If any of these links are broken or out of date please let me know. If you know of a site that needs to be linked here (your own or someone elses) let me know. My email is copehead@psychonaut.org

General Drug Information

  • The Lycaeum is probably the best source online for visionary plant and chemical information.

  • Erowid (or use this mirror) is definitely a close second, and one of my favorite sites.

  • The Essential Psychedelic Guide (and a mirror) is probably the best handbook for trippers I have ever seen, and thanks to the gods, is available online.

  • The Deoxyribonucleic Hyperdimension Lots of good info here, and some of the most beautiful HTML and images I have ever seen.

  • MAPS Inc. Multidisciplinary Association for Psychedelic Studies. Very cool group.

  • Peter Meyer's Psychedelic pages cover mostly tryptamines.

  • SpiritPlants.com is an small but well done site with a number of interesting articles and relevant news.

Specific Plants/Chemicals

  • Ecstasy.org (for original without HTTPS click here) Empathogenic fun with MDMA.

  • Daniel Siebert's Salvia Divinorum pages (salvia.lycaeum.org also links to sagewisdom.org) are an excellent source for all sorts of Salvia info.

  • Salvinorin: The Psychedelic Essence of Salvia Divinorum is also available online now! DM Turner's groundbreaking work on the usage of the active principle of Salvia.

  • The The Shroomery at shroomery.lycaeum.org now resiedes at shroomery.org. The Shroomery is an excellent compendium of magic mushroom info.

  • The Fane of the Psilocybe Mushroom is a fourth way school that uses the Mushroom to explore consciousness and mystical experience.

  • The Ibogaine Dossier contains info on this rare compound and its uses.

  • The Ayahuasca home page (the original ayahuasca.lycaeum.org moved to ayahuasca.com) is a great source for all sorts of info on this visionary brew, its users and uses.

Interesting Folks

  • Timothy Leary's Home Page Whether you love him or hate him, you can't deny he's interesting.

  • John C. Lilly Homepage And this guy makes Leary look normal! (Whatever that means...)

  • Terence McKenna is another psychedelic researcher with some wild ideas. Some wild ideas that insist on showing up in all my mushroom voyages...

  • Robert Anton Wilson If you don't know who Robert Anton Wilson is, it's time to find out.

  • Church of Gnostic Luminism is an interesting entheogenic church. There are some very cool writings here I haven't seen elsewhere.

  • Shamanism and the Drug Propaganda is an interesting site for a book of the same name.

War on (Some) Drugs

  • Peter Meyer's WoD pages are a good place to start.

  • Alchemind Society is a group dedicated to the principle that the freedom to control one's own consciousness is the essential foundation of all other freedoms. I like that!

Entheogen Inspired Art
